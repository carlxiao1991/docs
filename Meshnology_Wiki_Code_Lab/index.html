Meshnology Wiki & Code Lab

Meshnology was born from a group of passionate makers, engineers, and tinkerers who sought to redefine open-source hardware. What started as a workshop of exploration has now become a movement towards accessible and limitless innovation

You can find information about Meshnology’s N30 series for LoRa communication products, example code, and case studies in the Meshnology Wiki & Code Lab. It also features resources and sample programs for the Heltec ESP32 LoRa V3 development board, the RAK19007 WisBlock Base, and the RAK4631 module.
